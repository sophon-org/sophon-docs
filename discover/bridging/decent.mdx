You can bridge assets to Sophon using [Decent](https://decent.xyz/), a fast and expansive cross-chain API that also hosts their own bridge.

## How to Bridge

1. Visit [Decent](https://app.decent.xyz/decent) and connect your wallet.
2. Select your source chain and the token you want to send (Decent supports any token).
3. Select **Sophon** as your destination chain and **SOPH** as the token you want.
4. Enter your **desired amount**
5. Confirm the transaction and approve it in your wallet.
7. Your **SOPH** tokens will arrive on the destination chain shortly.
