## How to bridge SOPH

**Sophon <> Ethereum**

1. Canonical bridge
2. Orbiter
3. Layerswap

**Sophon <> Other chains**
1. Stargate

**Sophon <> CEX**

Your CEX should allow direct deposit from/to Sophon

**Bridging** lets you move assets between different blockchains, making it easier to use your tokens across ecosystems. Sophon currently offers multiple options when it comes to bridging:
