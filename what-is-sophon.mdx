**Sophon** is an **EVM-compatible Layer 2** ([Atlas-era zk stack](https://paragraph.com/@zksync/introducing-the-zk-stack-s-atlas-upgrade-beyond-15k-tps-with-one-second-zk-finality)) built for consumer UX. It pairs a lifestyle app (Aura), a premium stablecoin card, and a zkTLS-powered **Social Oracle** that turns verified digital signals into utility, without exposing raw data.

## Key Features of Sophon

### 1. **Consumer-first L2**

Fast, low-fee, fully EVM: standard tooling, familiar contracts.

Built for **products**, not pilots: optimized for **payments**, **high-frequency reads/writes**, and **simple integration paths.**

<Warning> Sophon no longer relies on **native AA** at the protocol layer. AA-style UX (sponsored gas, session keys) is provided via our SDKs and SSO at the **integration layer**. </Warning>

### 2. **Social Oracle**

Bring real-world signals onchain as proofs:

Verify traits like **creator reach**, **fitness activity**, **travel history**, **onchain behavior*, or any traits.

Partners query eligibility (e.g., “meets criteria”) without accessing personal details.

<Note> **Prove, don’t expose**: traits are zero-knowledge proofs you can revoke at any time. </Note>

### 3. **Sophon Account & SSO**

Sophon Account abstracts setup and key management behind **social/passkey sign-in**.

Our **SSO** provide AA-style flows without protocol changes.

### 4 **Payments & Card**

**Stablecoin** card that ties spend to gamified upside.

App + card loop: verified traits → perks → spending → upgraded access.

## How It Fits Together

**App:** build your **Aura** (private, verified digital DNA) from connected signals: unlock status, perks, and rewards.

**Card:** everyday spend on stablecoin rails: upside mechanics instead of fixed cashback.

**Chain:** EVM L2 where settlement, trait verification, and partner activations run end-to-end.

## For Users

**Privacy by default:** connect only what you want and revoke anytime.

**Immediate utility:** perks based on verified traits. Spend with the card.


## For Builders & Partners

**SSO:** For sign-in, sponsored flows, and session keys.

**Social Oracle:** APIs to target verified cohorts (eligibility checks, not data dumps).
